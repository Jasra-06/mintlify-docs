## Getting started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the program overview">
    Familiarize yourself with the internship structure, key dates, and what's expected of you. Start at [Program Overview](/program-overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Work through the [Onboarding Guide](/guides/onboarding) to set up access, meet your mentor, and get your environment ready.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand expectations">
    Review [Expectations](/guides/expectations) to know how your work will be evaluated and what milestones to hit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your project">
    Dive into [Project Guidelines](/guides/projects) to understand deliverables, timelines, and how to ask for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
