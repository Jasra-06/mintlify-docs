## The Onboarding Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="📝 Fill the Internship Form">
    Your very first action — submit the official registration form.

    <Card title="Open Registration Form →" icon="file-pen" href="https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=S96OfXB-mEWkdQX4GMsIY8wV57xX3DFDm7YpCbDXPORUNVZSNVBZSDJTM01RSkU4RFlVWTFMSlRIMy4u">
      Takes under 5 minutes to complete.
    </Card>

    <Warning>
      Do not move to the next step until this form is submitted.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="💬 Join Microsoft Teams">
    Teams is your daily hub — meetings, updates, mentor comms, announcements.

    - Sign in with `yourname.hink@outlook.com`
    - Join all assigned intern channels
    - Set status to **Available** and enable notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="📚 Study the Prerequisites">
    8 key topics to understand before contributing:

    - EdTech · LMS · SaaS
    - HashInkludes · TeachGrid
    - The Problem TeachGrid Solves
    - Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)
    - Competitor Research

    <Card title="Go to Prerequisites →" icon="book-open" href="/get-started/prerequisites">
      Detailed breakdown of every topic.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="📂 Review Resources">
    - Read the reference PDF shared by your mentor
    - Explore internal docs
    - Save PPT templates for your presentation

    <Card title="Go to Resources →" icon="folder-open" href="/get-started/resources">
      All materials in one place.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🔍 Explore TeachGrid App & CRM">
    Go hands-on with the actual product — this is essential.

    | Platform | Access |
    | --- | --- |
    | **TeachGrid App** | [teachgrid.io](https://teachgrid.io/) |
    | **TeachGrid CRM** | Credentials from your mentor |

    <Info>
      Spend 2–3 hours exploring. The better you know the product, the more impact you'll make.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🎤 Prepare Your First-Week Presentation">
    Every intern delivers a **15-minute presentation** at the end of Week 1.

    - Cover all 11 required topics
    - Build a clean, visual slide deck
    - Submit to mentor at least **1 hour before**
    - Be authentic — not just polished

    <Card title="Presentation Guide →" icon="presentation-screen" href="/get-started/first-week-presentation">
      Topics, tips, and slide structure.
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>
