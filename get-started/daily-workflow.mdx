## 🕙 Daily Intern Meet — 10:00 AM

**Mandatory.** 20–30 minutes, every day on Teams.

- Join by **9:58 AM** — camera on, mic ready
- Share what you worked on, what's next, and any blockers
- Listen actively — updates from others help you too

<Warning>
  Can't make it? DM your mentor **before 10:00 AM**. Repeated no-shows are taken seriously.
</Warning>

## 📋 EOD Update — 5:30 PM

Post this in Teams every day. Copy the template below:

```text
Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

Tasks Completed:
- ...

Learnings:
- ...

Blockers Faced:
- ...

Pending Work:
- ...
```

<Tip>
  Keep notes throughout the day — at 5:30 PM, paste and send. Done in 2 minutes.
</Tip>

## Communication Standards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="✅ Be Professional in Every Message" icon="user-tie">
    - No slang in work channels
    - Proofread before sending
    - Respond within 2 hours during working hours
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="⚠️ Communicate Blockers Early" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    - Flag issues at the daily meet or DM your mentor right away
    - Asking for help is smart — staying silent is not
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🤝 Collaborate with Fellow Interns" icon="people-group">
    - Share learnings and useful resources in channels
    - This is a team experience — grow together
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Consistency is your superpower. Show up, communicate clearly, submit your updates — and you'll stand out effortlessly.
</Note>
