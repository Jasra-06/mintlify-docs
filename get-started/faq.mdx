## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who do I contact when I have doubts?" icon="user-tie">
    **Your assigned mentor** is your #1 contact for tasks, docs, feedback, and tech issues.

    If unavailable, reach out to your team lead or program coordinator.

    <Tip>
      Never stay stuck in silence. Asking is always faster than guessing.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this internship paid or stipend-based?" icon="circle-dollar-to-slot">
    This internship is primarily designed as a **learning and growth opportunity** — where interns explore real startup workflows, industry concepts, collaboration, and hands-on experience in the EdTech ecosystem.

    The focus of the program is on **skill development, learning exposure, and practical experience**. We are committed to making this a genuinely valuable and enriching journey for every intern who joins us.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How quickly should I respond to messages?" icon="clock">
    - **Teams messages:** Within 2 hours during working hours
    - **Emails:** Within 24 hours on working days
    - **Outside work hours:** Not required immediately

    Give your mentor a heads-up if you'll be unavailable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I communicate professionally?" icon="comments">
    - Clear subject lines, concise body, proofread before sending
    - Join meetings 2 minutes early, camera on
    - **Golden rule:** When in doubt, be slightly more formal
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Submissions & Assignments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I submit assignments?" icon="upload">
    Your mentor will specify per task. Common channels:

    - Project tool (Notion / Trello / ClickUp)
    - Email to mentor — for formal deliverables
    - Shared Drive / OneDrive — for docs and files

    **When in doubt, ask before submitting.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I submit my presentation?" icon="presentation-screen">
    1. Send deck (PPT / Google Slides / PDF) to mentor **at least 1 hour before**
    2. Subject line: `[Your Name] — First Week Presentation`

    <Warning>
      Never show up without submitting the deck in advance.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the deadline policy?" icon="calendar">
    - Need an extension → request **24 hours before**
    - Blocked → communicate immediately
    - Missed without notice → flag and explain ASAP

    Proactive communication is always respected. Silence is not.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Platforms & Tools

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What platforms will I use?" icon="desktop">
    | Platform | Purpose |
    | --- | --- |
    | **TeachGrid App** | Core learning platform |
    | **TeachGrid CRM** | Lead and client management |
    | **Microsoft Teams** | Daily communication and meetings |
    | **Outlook Email** | Official work email |

    Credentials are shared during orientation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get TeachGrid CRM access?" icon="key">
    Your mentor shares credentials during onboarding. If you haven't received them by end of Day 1, DM your mentor.

    **Do not share credentials with anyone.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I miss the 10 AM daily meet?" icon="video">
    - DM your mentor **before 10:00 AM** with a reason
    - Catch up with a teammate on what was covered
    - Don't let it become a pattern — attendance is mandatory

    <Warning>
      Repeated unexcused absences are taken seriously.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What do I do if I hit a technical problem?" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    1. Refresh, clear cache, restart browser
    2. Try a different device or browser
    3. Report to mentor with: description, screenshots, error message
    4. Mentor escalates if platform-level

    <Tip>
      More detail = faster resolution.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
