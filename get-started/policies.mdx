<Note>
  🤝 These aren't corporate rules — they're the norms of a high-performing startup team. They exist to support you, not restrict you.
</Note>

> _Show up. Communicate. Do good work. Be kind. Ask for help early. Never stop learning._

---

## Attendance 🗓️

- Attend all meetings, standups, and presentations
- Notify your mentor **at least 1–2 hours before** if you can't make it
- Catch up independently on anything missed

<Warning>
  Repeated unexcused absences may result in a formal review. Going silent is never the right move.
</Warning>

---

## Communication 💬

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="✅ Do This">
    - Clear, concise language in all messages
    - Respond within 2 hrs (Teams) / 24 hrs (email)
    - Camera on for video calls
    - Proofread before sending
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="❌ Avoid This">
    - Slang or casual tone in work channels
    - Going silent without explanation
    - Sharing confidential info externally
    - Dismissive or one-word responses
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

---

## Deadlines ⏰

| Situation | What to Do |
| --- | --- |
| On track | Deliver great work |
| Need an extension | Ask **24 hours before** |
| Facing a blocker | Communicate immediately |
| Already missed | Flag and explain ASAP |
