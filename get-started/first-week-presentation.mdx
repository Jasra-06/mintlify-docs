<Note>
  🎤 Every intern delivers a **15-minute presentation** at the end of Week 1. This is your moment — own it.
</Note>

## The Basics

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 15 minutes |
| **Format** | PPT, Google Slides, or Canva |
| **Audience** | Your team and mentors |
| **Tone** | Honest, creative, authentic |
| **Submit deck** | At least **1 hour before** your slot |

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="✅ It IS" icon="circle-check">
    A conversation. A space to share your fresh perspective — honestly and boldly.
  </Card>

  <Card title="❌ It Is NOT" icon="circle-xmark">
    A test. Don't say what you think we want to hear — be real.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  We value **honesty and curiosity** over perfection. Your fresh perspective is exactly what we want.
</Info>

---

## 11 Topics to Cover 📝

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1 — What is HashInkludes? (In your words)" icon="building">
    Mission, what they build, where they fit. Use your own words — not the website copy.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2 — What is TeachGrid? (In your words)" icon="globe">
    Explain it to a friend who's never heard of it. Purpose, features, who it serves.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="3 — EdTech Market & Trends" icon="chart-line">
    - What trends are shaping EdTech right now?
    - How is AI changing learning?
    - What surprised you about this space?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="4 — What You Learned in Week 1" icon="brain">
    New concepts, TeachGrid observations, skills you started building.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="5 — Your Internship Experience So Far" icon="star">
    What's been exciting? Challenging? Your honest first impression.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="6 — Feedback & Suggestions" icon="comments">
    What could onboarding improve? Anything confusing? Any ideas?

    **Be honest — we act on good feedback.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="7 — TeachGrid's Mission & Vision" icon="target">
    What problem does it solve? Who benefits? Where is it headed?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="8 — Product Observations" icon="lightbulb">
    What do you like? What feels well-designed? What feels incomplete?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="9 — Possible Improvements" icon="wrench">
    UI/UX suggestions, missing features, workflow improvements. **Be bold.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="10 — How You Plan to Contribute" icon="hands-helping">
    Skills you bring, what you're excited to work on, what you want to master.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="11 — What Excites You About Being Here" icon="heart">
    End on a genuine high — about the product, the culture, or your own growth.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Suggested Slide Structure 🗂️

| Slide | Content |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Title \+ Your Name |
| 2–3 | HashInkludes & TeachGrid (in your words) |
| 4–5 | EdTech Market Observations |
| 6–7 | Week 1 Learnings \+ TeachGrid Exploration |
| 8 | Internship Experience So Far |
| 9 | Product Observations & Improvements |
| 10 | Feedback & Suggestions |
| 11 | How You'll Contribute \+ What Excites You |
| 12 | Thank You \+ Q&A |

---

## Readiness Checklist ✅

- All 11 topics covered in deck
- Deck is visual — no walls of text
- Practiced out loud and timed (12–15 min)
- Deck submitted to mentor 1 hr before
- Being authentic — not just polished

<Tip>
  💡 The best presentations are honest ones. Tell us what you actually think — that's what makes them memorable.
</Tip>
