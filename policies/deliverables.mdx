## Required Deliverables

| Deliverable | Frequency | Due |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **EOD Update** | Daily | 5:30 PM every working day |
| **First Week Presentation** | Week 1 | End of Week 1 |
| **Task Submissions** | Per assignment | As specified by mentor |

---

## EOD Update (Daily) 📋

Post this in Microsoft Teams every day by **5:30 PM** — no exceptions.

```text
Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

Tasks Completed:
- ...

Learnings:
- ...

Blockers Faced:
- ...

Pending Work:
- ...
```

<Tip>
  Keep notes throughout the day. At 5:30 PM, paste and send. Done in 2 minutes.
</Tip>

## Quality Standards

All submissions should be:

- ✅ **Clear** — easy to read, no fluff
- ✅ **Specific** — concrete details, not vague summaries
- ✅ **Professional** — proofread, correct grammar
- ✅ **On time** — or communicated early if delayed
