## Daily Expectations

| Time | What's Expected |
| --- | --- |
| **Working hours** | Monday–Friday, aligned with your team timezone |
| **10:00 AM** | 🔴 Daily Intern Meet — mandatory, camera on |
| **5:30 PM** | 📋 EOD Update submitted in Teams |
| **Throughout day** | Available on Teams, respond within 2 hours |

---

## This is a Remote Internship

The HashInkludes internship is conducted **fully remote via Microsoft Teams**.

- Be logged into Teams and visible during working hours
- Keep camera on during all meetings
- Ensure stable internet and a quiet workspace
- Proactively share your status — remote = more intentional communication

---

## Absence Notification

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="📅 Planned Absence">
    - Notify your mentor **at least 24–48 hours before**
    - Share your status on any pending tasks
    - Don't schedule planned absences during critical milestones (e.g., presentation week)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="🤒 Unplanned Absence">
    - DM your mentor **as early as possible on the same morning**
    - One short message is all it takes — "I'm unwell and won't be online today"
    - Follow up when you're back
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="⏰ Late to Daily Meet">
    - DM your mentor **before 10:00 AM**
    - Catch up with a fellow intern on what was discussed
    - Repeated late arrivals are noted by mentors
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Going silent without any communication is never acceptable — even in genuine emergencies, a quick message goes a long way.
</Warning>

## Quick Reference

| Situation | Notice Required | Who to Notify |
