<Note>
  🤝 These standards exist to protect you and your teammates. They're not rules to fear — they're the foundation of a great team culture.
</Note>

> _Be the kind of person you'd want to work with: reliable, respectful, curious, and honest._

---

## Core Standards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="✅ Professionalism & Respect" icon="handshake">
    Treat every person — colleagues, mentors, fellow interns — with dignity and respect in all interactions (meetings, Teams, email, DMs).

    - Come prepared to meetings
    - Use inclusive language
    - Listen actively — don't interrupt or dismiss
    - Own your mistakes and ask for help
    - Represent HashInkludes professionally in every message you send
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🔒 Confidentiality" icon="lock">
    Everything you encounter at HashInkludes — product plans, client data, strategies, pricing, internal docs — is **strictly confidential**.

    - Do not share internal info externally (including family, friends, social media)
    - Do not store company data on personal cloud accounts
    - Do not share login credentials with anyone
    - These obligations continue **after** the internship ends

    **When in doubt — don't share. Ask your mentor first.**
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📱 Social Media" icon="share-2">
    **You may share:**

    - That you're interning at HashInkludes
    - General reflections on skills and learning
    - Your own growth and experience

    **You must NOT share:**

    - Unreleased features or products
    - Internal screenshots, data, or documents
    - Negative commentary about the team or company
    - Client or pricing information

    <Warning>
      When in doubt, ask before posting. Social media violations are treated as confidentiality breaches.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🚩 Reporting Concerns" icon="flag">
    If something feels wrong — a teammate interaction, a task, a situation — report it.

    1. Speak with your mentor directly (if comfortable)
    2. If it involves your mentor, contact the program coordinator
    3. All reports are handled confidentially and taken seriously

    **You will never be penalized for raising a legitimate concern in good faith.**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## The Short Version

| Do ✅ | Don't ❌ |
| --- | --- |
| Be respectful in all interactions | Dismiss or interrupt teammates |
| Keep all company info confidential | Share internal data externally |
| Ask before you're unsure | Stay silent when something's wrong |
| Own your mistakes | Blame others or go quiet |
| Be present and consistent | Ghost the team without notice |
