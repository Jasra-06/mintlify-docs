<Note>
  🔧 Try the steps below first. If still stuck, DM your mentor with a screenshot and a description of the issue.
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I haven't received my Outlook credentials — what do I do?" icon="envelope">
    1. Check your personal email spam/junk folder
    2. Look for an email from Microsoft or HashInkludes with your `yourname.hink@outlook.com` invite
    3. Still nothing? DM your mentor or program coordinator immediately with your full name and registered personal email
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't sign in to Microsoft Teams" icon="comments">
    1. Confirm you're using `yourname.hink@outlook.com` — not your personal email
    2. Try resetting your password at [account.microsoft.com](https://account.microsoft.com)
    3. Clear browser cache or try a different browser
    4. Download the **Teams desktop app** — it works better than the browser version
    5. If still stuck, DM your mentor from any available channel
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't access TeachGrid or the CRM" icon="globe">
    - **TeachGrid App:** Visit [teachgrid.io](https://teachgrid.io) and sign up with your work email
    - **TeachGrid CRM:** Credentials are provided by your mentor — DM them if you haven't received them by end of Day 1
    - **Do not share credentials** with anyone
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My Teams notifications aren't working" icon="bell">
    1. Open Teams → **Settings → Notifications** → enable all relevant alerts
    2. Check your OS notification permissions:
       - **Windows:** Settings → System → Notifications → Teams ✅
       - **Mac:** System Settings → Notifications → Teams ✅
    3. Turn off Do Not Disturb / Focus mode
    4. Restart the Teams app completely

    <Warning>
      Missing notifications means missing updates from your mentor. Fix this on Day 1.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I missed the 10 AM Daily Meet — what do I do?" icon="video">
    1. DM your mentor **immediately** to explain
    2. Ask a fellow intern what was covered
    3. Catch up on any announcements in Teams channels
    4. Don't let it become a pattern — attendance is mandatory
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm having a technical issue with TeachGrid" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    1. Refresh the page and clear browser cache
    2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
    3. If it persists — message your mentor with:
       - Screenshot of the error
       - What you were trying to do
       - Your browser and device type

    <Tip>
      More detail = faster fix. Vague reports slow everything down.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I got locked out of my Outlook account" icon="lock">
    1. Go to [account.microsoft.com](https://account.microsoft.com) and click **Forgot password**
    2. Verify via your personal email (the one used during signup)
    3. If you can't recover it yourself, DM your mentor — they'll escalate to the program coordinator
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What do I do if I accidentally share something confidential?" icon="shield-exclamation">
    Report it to your mentor **immediately** — even if it feels minor.

    All information about HashInkludes, TeachGrid, clients, and strategies is strictly confidential. Honest and prompt reporting is always the right call.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
