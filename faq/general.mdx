</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who do I contact when I need help?" icon="user-tie">
    **Your assigned mentor** is your #1 contact — for tasks, questions, feedback, and blockers.

    If they're unavailable, reach out to the program coordinator. Never stay stuck in silence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this internship paid or stipend-based?" icon="circle-dollar-to-slot">
    This internship is primarily designed as a **learning and growth opportunity** — where interns explore real startup workflows, industry concepts, collaboration, and hands-on experience in the EdTech ecosystem.

    The focus of the program is on **skill development, learning exposure, and practical experience**. We are committed to making this a genuinely valuable and enriching journey for every intern who joins us.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are my working hours?" icon="clock">
    The standard schedule is **Monday–Friday**, aligned with your team's timezone.

    - Daily Intern Meet at **10:00 AM** is mandatory
    - EOD Update by **5:30 PM** every day
    - Be reachable on Teams during working hours

    Confirm specifics with your mentor on Day 1.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does a typical day look like?" icon="calendar-day">
    | Time | Activity |
    | --- | --- |
    | Morning | Check Outlook \+ Teams |
    | 10:00 AM | Daily Intern Meet (mandatory) |
    | 10:30 AM\+ | Tasks, TeachGrid exploration, learning |
    | 5:30 PM | Submit EOD Update |

    See the [Daily Workflow](/get-started/daily-workflow) page for the full breakdown.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is this internship remote?" icon="house">
    Yes — the HashInkludes internship is conducted remotely via Microsoft Teams.

    You're expected to be online and available during working hours, with camera on for all meetings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I need to take time off?" icon="calendar">
    - Planned absence → notify mentor **24–48 hours before**
    - Unplanned absence (illness, emergency) → DM your mentor **same morning**
    - Missing the 10 AM meet → DM mentor **before 10 AM**

    Proactive communication is always respected. Going silent is not.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is my performance evaluated?" icon="star">
    HashInkludes mentors observe:

    - **Attendance & consistency** — showing up, daily meets, EOD updates
    - **Learning & curiosity** — how deeply you engage with prerequisites and tasks
    - **Communication** — clarity, professionalism, and proactiveness
    - **Contribution** — quality and initiative in your assigned work

    Feedback is shared at the end of the program.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I list this internship on my resume/LinkedIn?" icon="linkedin">
    Yes! You're encouraged to share your experience professionally.

    You may mention:

    - Your role and team at HashInkludes
    - Skills and tools you worked with
    - Projects or contributions (general descriptions)

    **Do not share** internal data, pricing, client info, or unreleased product details publicly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
