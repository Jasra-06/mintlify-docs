<Note>
  ⚡ Follow these steps in order. Each one builds on the last. Complete them all by end of Week 1.
</Note>

## Your Onboarding Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="📧 Activate Your Work Email">
    - Go to [outlook.com](https://outlook.com) and sign in with `yourname.hink@outlook.com`
    - Set a strong password and complete account setup
    - **Use this for all work communication — no personal email**
  </Step>
  <Step title="📝 Submit the Internship Registration Form">
    Do this before anything else.

    <Card title="Open Registration Form →" icon="file-pen" href="https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=S96OfXB-mEWkdQX4GMsIY8wV57xX3DFDm7YpCbDXPORUNVZSNVBZSDJTM01RSkU4RFlVWTFMSlRIMy4u">
      Takes under 5 minutes.
    </Card>

    <Warning>
      Do not proceed until this form is submitted.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="💬 Join Microsoft Teams">
    - Download the Teams desktop app at [teams.microsoft.com](https://teams.microsoft.com)
    - Sign in with `yourname.hink@outlook.com`
    - Join all assigned intern channels
    - Set status to **Available**, enable notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="📚 Study All 8 Prerequisites">
    Before your first meeting, learn these topics:

    - EdTech · LMS · SaaS
    - HashInkludes · TeachGrid
    - The Problem TeachGrid Solves
    - Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)
    - Competitor Research

    <Card title="Go to Prerequisites →" icon="book-open" href="/get-started/prerequisites">
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="📄 Read the Mandatory Reference Document">
    The **EdTech & LMS Fundamentals PDF** is required reading before your first intern meeting.

    <Card title="Open Reference PDF →" icon="file-pdf" href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jasra-06/internship-docs/main/EdTech%20%26%20LMS%20Fundamentals.pdf">
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🔍 Explore TeachGrid App & CRM">
    | Platform | How to Access |
    | --- | --- |
    | **TeachGrid App** | [teachgrid.io](https://teachgrid.io/) |
    | **TeachGrid CRM** | Credentials from your mentor |

    Spend at least **2–3 hours** exploring. The better you know the product, the more you'll contribute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="🕙 Attend Daily 10 AM Meet">
    Every morning on Teams — mandatory.

    - Join by **9:58 AM**, camera on
    - Submit **EOD update** every day by 5:30 PM

    <Card title="Daily Workflow Guide →" icon="clock" href="/get-started/daily-workflow">
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🎤 Prepare & Deliver First Week Presentation">
    - Cover all 11 required topics
    - Build a visual slide deck (PPT / Canva / Google Slides)
    - Submit to mentor **1 hour before** your slot
    - Be authentic — it's a conversation, not a test

    <Card title="Presentation Guide →" icon="presentation-screen" href="/get-started/first-week-presentation">
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>
