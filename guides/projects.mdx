Interns at HashInkludes contribute to **real startup work** — not simulated tasks. You'll be assigned work based on your skills and interests, and you'll collaborate with your mentor throughout.

## How to Do Great Work 🚀

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understand the task before starting">
    Read the brief fully. If anything is unclear, ask your mentor before you begin — not after.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work in the open">
    Share early drafts, ask for feedback mid-way, and update your mentor daily. Don't disappear for days and then submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Flag blockers immediately">
    Stuck? Say so at the 10 AM meet or DM your mentor. Waiting costs everyone time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit on time — always">
    Need an extension? Ask **24 hours before the deadline**. Silence is not acceptable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for Your First Week Presentation 💡

- Open with **the problem** — context first, solution second
- Be honest about what surprised or confused you — that's valuable
- Use visuals — comparison tables, screenshots, diagrams
- Practice out loud at least once before presenting
- End with what excites you about being here — make it memorable

<Tip>
  The best presentations aren't the most polished — they're the most honest and thoughtful. Be real.
</Tip>
