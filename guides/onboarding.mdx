## Week 1 Onboarding Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="📧 Activate Outlook & Join Teams">
    - Sign in to `yourname.hink@outlook.com` at [outlook.com](https://outlook.com)
    - Download Microsoft Teams desktop app
    - Sign into Teams with your Outlook credentials
    - Join all intern channels your mentor shares
    - Set status to **Available**, enable notifications
  </Step>
  <Step title="📝 Submit the Internship Registration Form">
    This must be done before anything else.

    <Card title="Open Registration Form →" icon="file-pen" href="https://forms.office.com/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=S96OfXB-mEWkdQX4GMsIY8wV57xX3DFDm7YpCbDXPORUNVZSNVBZSDJTM01RSkU4RFlVWTFMSlRIMy4u">
      Takes under 5 minutes.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="📚 Study All 8 Prerequisites">
    Before your first meeting, study:

    - EdTech, LMS, SaaS
    - HashInkludes & TeachGrid
    - The Problem TeachGrid Solves
    - Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP)
    - Competitor Research

    <Card title="Go to Prerequisites →" icon="book-open" href="/get-started/prerequisites">
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="📄 Read the Reference PDF">
    The **EdTech & LMS Fundamentals PDF** is mandatory reading before your first intern meeting.

    <Card title="Download PDF →" icon="file-pdf" href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jasra-06/internship-docs/main/EdTech%20%26%20LMS%20Fundamentals.pdf">
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🔍 Explore TeachGrid App & CRM">
    - Visit [teachgrid.io](https://teachgrid.io) and explore as a learner and admin
    - Get CRM credentials from your mentor
    - Spend at least 2 hours exploring the platform

    <Info>
      The better you know TeachGrid, the more you'll contribute from Day 1.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="🕙 Attend the Daily 10 AM Meet">
    Every morning at **10:00 AM on Teams** — mandatory.

    - Join by 9:58 AM, camera on
    - Come with updates, questions, or blockers ready
    - Submit EOD update every day by **5:30 PM**
  </Step>
  <Step title="🎤 Prepare & Deliver First Week Presentation">
    By end of Week 1:

    - Cover all 11 required topics
    - Build a visual slide deck (PPT / Canva / Google Slides)
    - Submit to mentor **1 hour before** your slot
    - Present authentically — it's a conversation, not a test

    <Card title="Presentation Guide →" icon="presentation-screen" href="/get-started/first-week-presentation">
    </Card>
  </Step>
</Steps>
