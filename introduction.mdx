## 🚀 Start Your Internship Journey

Complete these 6 steps in order during your first week:

<Steps>
  <Step title="📝 Fill the Internship Form">
    Your very first action. Submit the official registration before anything else.
  </Step>
  <Step title="💬 Join Microsoft Teams">
    Sign in with `yourname.hink@outlook.com` and join all assigned intern channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="📚 Study the Prerequisites">
    Learn 8 core concepts — EdTech, LMS, SaaS, TeachGrid, ICPs, competitors, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="📄 Review the Reference Document">
    Read the EdTech & LMS Fundamentals PDF (in Resources) before your first meeting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="🕙 Attend the Daily Intern Meet">
    Every morning at **10:00 AM** on Teams. Camera on. Come prepared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="🎤 Prepare Your First Week Presentation">
    Deliver a 15-minute presentation at the end of Week 1. It's your moment — own it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
